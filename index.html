Welcome!

I am currently a third-year graduate student in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA. I am part of the Cementitious Materials Research Group, supervised by Dr. Aleksandra Radlińska. I received my B.Eng. from Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China.

My research interests lie in the development, evaluation, and implementation of advanced concrete materials by using computational and experimental approaches.
